Who We Are?

We are a team of highly skilled and experienced IB teachers who have come together to help students reach their true potential by channelling their efforts in the right direction. We do this with excellent classroom teaching, continuous feedback and mentoring.

Our expert teachers are not only subject experts but also highly experienced in terms of the IB and IGCSE assessment patterns. With the help of our team, students can achieve their true potential. The future holds a competitive environment and individuals with high skills can survive the challenges. IB and IGCSE are excellent curriculums, and with criteria based and focused teaching support that we at aimIB7™ provide, students are sure to reach academic excellence. Our batch sizes are never more than 5 to ensure differentiated instruction and personalized mentoring. Our teaching partners have proven teaching records and we can assure IB experienced and senior teachers, who will provide the most accurate guidance.

The teaching materials are developed at our end and practice papers modelled at the IB and IGCSE patterns by our faculty. IA and Essay writing support is also done by multiple faculties at aimIB7™.
